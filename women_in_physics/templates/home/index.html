Women in Physics Lesson

The goal of this website is to allow students to give completely anonymous responses to survey questions about difficulties you might have faced in your classes. Teachers can choose to share these responses to the student dashboards or to keep them private, but at no point will a name be associated with a response unless you choose to add it.

If you are a teacher, you can create a class for your students to use or login to the class you have already made.

If you are a student, you can login using the username that your teacher gave to your class.
